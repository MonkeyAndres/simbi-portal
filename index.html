What is Simbi?

A digital organism that lives on your machine. It learns who you are through conversation — your preferences, your people, your patterns.

It remembers. It grows. It gets better every day. And it belongs entirely to you.

No cloud. No accounts. No tracking. Everything lives on your device.

👁️ Attention filter

🔒 Self-hosted & yours

Living code that adapts to you. It grows with you because it grows from you.
